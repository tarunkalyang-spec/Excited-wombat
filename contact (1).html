Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment?

While appointments are not required, we recommend scheduling one to ensure a sales consultant is available to give you their full attention.

Can I trade in my current vehicle?

Absolutely! We accept all trade-ins and offer fair market value. Bring your vehicle and we'll provide a free appraisal.

Do you deliver vehicles?

Yes! We offer delivery services within a 100-mile radius. Contact us for details and pricing.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept cash, certified checks, financing through our lenders, and can work with your bank or credit union.

Do you offer warranties?

Yes! We offer extended warranty options on most vehicles. Ask your sales consultant for details.

Can I see a vehicle history report?

Every vehicle comes with a free CARFAX or AutoCheck report. We believe in complete transparency.
