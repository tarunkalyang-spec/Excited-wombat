Our Story

AutoPremium was founded in 2005 with a simple mission: to provide quality vehicles and exceptional customer service to our community. What started as a small family-owned lot has grown into one of the region's most trusted dealerships.

For nearly two decades, we've been helping families find their perfect vehicles. We believe that buying a car should be an exciting experience, not a stressful one. That's why we've built our business on honesty, transparency, and putting our customers first.

Today, we're proud to offer a wide selection of quality pre-owned vehicles, competitive financing options, and a service department that keeps your vehicle running smoothly for years to come. Our commitment to excellence has earned us thousands of satisfied customers and countless referrals.
